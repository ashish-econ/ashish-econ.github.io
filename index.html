Hi, welcome to my webpage!

My name is Christine Cai and I'm currently a first-year Ph.D. Candidate in Economics at Princeton University.

My research interests (which are subject to changes...!) currently lie in applied microeconomics (this broader subfield is unlikely to change though!) and in particular, education/labor, development, and urban economics. I am notably interested in issues related to educational inequalities, social mobility, and segregation.

I previously worked as a full-time research assistant for Professor Rebecca Dizon-Ross at the University of Chicago Booth School of Business (2016-2018) and for Professor Ruben Durante at the Department of Economics of Sciences Po Paris (2015-2016).

I hold a Master's degree in Economics as well as a Bachelor's degree in Economics, both from Sciences Po Paris.
